Dan River Window Company is a family-owned window manufacturer based in South Boston, VA that has been producing custom vinyl windows since 1999. The site was built to present their two product lines — the Classic Series and Dan-Weld Series — and convert both homeowners and contractors into direct inquiries.

The project focused on communicating the company's factory-direct value proposition: custom windows built in-house, installed by their own crew, within a 60-mile service radius. The site targets both residential customers seeking replacement windows and trade buyers looking for wholesale pricing.
