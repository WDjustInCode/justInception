Dunavant Engineering & Construction, Inc. is a third-generation family civil contractor established in 1965, operating throughout Southside Virginia. The site was built to establish a credible web presence for a company with deep regional roots and an active project roster spanning industrial, commercial, and institutional site work.

The project highlights the company's six decades of experience across grading and earthwork, excavation, asphalt paving, site utilities, demolition, and turnkey site development. Active projects at the time of launch included the Hitachi Energy facility, Sentara hospital, and Danville Regional Airport.
